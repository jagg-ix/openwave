# M9.59 finding

The baseline action reproduces strong untrapped spreading (`radius ratio 4.862`, boundary fraction `0.704`). The bounded cubic--quintic action term with `alpha=70`, `beta=380` reduces the radius ratio to `1.328` on the primary grid and retains localization on the 12³, 14³, and 16³ campaigns with boundary fractions below `0.01`. Particle stability therefore moves from a bounded negative to a bounded partial result. Continuum orbital stability, uniqueness of the action term, and physical calibration remain open.
