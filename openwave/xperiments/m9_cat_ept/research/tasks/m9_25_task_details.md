# M9.25 task: CAT/EPT-native localized-state search

Search a bounded dimensionless complex-time field sector for finite-norm,
decaying localized states. Static and dynamic gates must use the same field and
nonlinearity.

Required gates:

- convergent stationary residual;
- finite norm and decaying tails;
- bounded response to a fixed perturbation;
- long-horizon reversible persistence;
- norm closure;
- domain sensitivity;
- explicit separation between a continuous 1D family and a selected 3D particle.

A passing result may establish a localized reference family. It may not promote
a unique scale, physical mass, electric charge, or three-dimensional particle.
