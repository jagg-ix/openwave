# M9.27 task: rest-energy and scale selection

Introduce a complete dimensionless energy decomposition for a selected
topological ansatz and determine whether competing sectors produce an interior
scale minimum.

Required gates:

- numerical and analytic scale agreement;
- convergent radial quadrature;
- vanishing first derivative and positive curvature;
- relaxation toward the minimum from smaller and larger scales;
- Derrick controls showing that either missing sector removes the minimum;
- no promotion to physical mass or a three-dimensional particle.

The selected baby-Skyrme ansatz is a bounded scale-selection mechanism, not the
full CAT/EPT energy functional.
