# M9.26 task: field-derived topological charge

Implement an integer observable from the simulated field rather than from an
external source label.

Required gates:

- integer winding sectors;
- resolution and contour robustness;
- global-phase invariance;
- robustness under smooth perturbations;
- additivity for separated defects;
- rejection when the field vanishes on the measurement contour;
- explicit distinction between seeded topology and spontaneous sector selection.

The observable is not identified with elementary electric charge unless a
separate gauge and force-law bridge is established.
