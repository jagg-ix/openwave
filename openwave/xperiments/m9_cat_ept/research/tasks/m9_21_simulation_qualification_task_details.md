# M9.21 task: cross-theory simulation qualification

Add a common-scenario qualification harness for comparing a new theory with existing controls entirely inside simulation.

Required capabilities:

- frozen scenario and observable schema;
- adapter-level law checks;
- deterministic replay fingerprints;
- pairwise normalized trajectory distances;
- parameter sweeps and sensitivity checks;
- reversible control and irreversible candidates under identical time grids;
- explicit failure for duplicate or malformed adapters.

No apparatus, acquisition, detector, or physical-data workflow is in scope.
