# m4_ewt , theory citations

> **These documents are NOT in git.** This folder holds third-party copyrighted research
> papers / author docs, kept **local only** and gitignored (OpenWave is a public repo). This
> manifest is the tracked record; obtain the papers from their original venues / authors.
> Our own research notes (`.md`) and code remain tracked.

Total: 34 local-only documents (as of 2026-07-03).

m4_ewt (Energy Wave Theory) is the scientific-origin corpus for the wave-structure-of-matter lineage. The EWT library (Jeff Yee's numbered documents and supplementals, plus Butto's electron-spin paper) was consolidated here on 2026-07-03, relocated from `m3_wolff_lafreniere/theory/` because EWT is the origin the M3 model builds on; `../m3_wolff_lafreniere/theory/_CITATIONS.md` keeps its own citations and points at these relocated files. This folder also holds the M4 implementation document (Smoliński). Entries are ordered by year (ascending; undated EWT author docs last); the Bibliography is the readable list and the Local corpus appendix is the gitignored-file inventory (path + size).
